# Script #1 "Le vol Bangkok" — VERSION RÉVISÉE

**Platform:** Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts  
**Duration:** 30–33s  
**Language:** French  
**Target audience:** Digital nomads, frequent travelers (25–40 yo)  

---

## 🎬 Scene Breakdown with Psychology

### Scene 1: Hook (0–2s) — **Attention + Specificity**

**Voiceover (whispered, shocked):**
> "Paris–Bangkok. 312€. J'ai pas rêvé."

**On screen:**
- Extreme close-up of creator's shocked face holding phone
- Laptop screen shows flight booking confirmation (blur personal info)

**Psychology applied:**
- **Anchoring Effect:** Specific price (€312) creates immediate reference point
- **Disbelief trigger:** "J'ai pas rêvé" = skepticism → curiosity
- **Authenticity:** Whispered = caught-in-the-moment (vs. corporate tone)

---

### Scene 2: Proof & Contrast (2–8s) — **Loss Aversion + Availability Heuristic**

**Voiceover (explanatory, calm):**
> "Normalement c'est 850€. Là c'est une alerte GlobeGenius. Ils scannent 24h/24 pour les deals comme ça."

**On screen:**
- Split screen: left shows "€850" (crossed out), right shows "€312" 
- GlobeGenius notification pops up with animated effect
- Text overlay: "NORMALLY: €850 | NOW: €312 | SAVINGS: €538"

**Psychology applied:**
- **Contrast Effect:** €850 → €312 makes the deal feel huge
- **Social Proof implicit:** "GlobeGenius scans 24h/24" = algorithm authority
- **Proof-moment:** Visual confirmation removes skepticism
- **Loss Aversion:** €538 savings = concrete loss-avoidance motivation

---

### Scene 3: Speed Proof (8–16s) — **Scarcity + Urgency**

**Voiceover (normal pace, slightly emphatic):**
> "J'ai réservé en 4 minutes. Trois heures après? Plus dispo. C'est comme ça avec les deals erreur."

**On screen:**
- Quick cuts (2–3s each):
  - Hands on keyboard, booking confirmation
  - Timer showing "3h later — EXPIRED" ❌
  - Creator thumbs up gesture
  - Suitcase, passport montage

**Psychology applied:**
- **Scarcity:** "Plus dispo" (gone) = concrete deadline proof
- **Urgency:** 3-hour window = real FOMO, not manufactured
- **Speed proof:** 4 minutes = not just fast, but *decisive*
- **Lindy Effect:** "C'est comme ça avec les deals erreur" = legitimizes the scarcity

---

### Scene 4: Lifestyle + Nomadic Identity (16–22s) — **Mimetic Desire + Social Proof**

**Voiceover (warm, conversational):**
> "Si tu fais comme moi—tu voyages souvent—tu dois tester. Les nomades adorent ça."

**On screen:**
- Montage (3–4s each):
  - Creator in café (working)
  - Airport security line
  - Beach / destination reveal
  - Creator smiling at camera

**Psychology applied:**
- **Mimetic Desire:** "Nomads love this" = social proof from your tribe
- **Identity alignment:** "If you travel like me" = similarity bias
- **Lifestyle aspiration:** Destination montage = "This could be you"
- **Unity principle:** Positioning GlobeGenius as *nomad tool*, not generic app

---

### Scene 5: CTA (22–30s) — **Reciprocity + Low Friction**

**Voiceover (encouraging, smile in voice):**
> "Gratuit. Zéro prise de tête. Lien en bio."

**On screen:**
- Text fades in: "GLOBEGENIUS.APP | LIEN EN BIO"
- GlobeGenius logo animation
- Quick flash of "Free tier" badge

**Psychology applied:**
- **Zero-Price Effect:** "Gratuit" (free) is psychologically different from "cheap"
- **Reciprocity:** Free tier creates obligation to try
- **Low activation energy:** "Zéro prise de tête" = removes friction objections
- **Hyperbolic Discounting:** "Try now" (immediate) > "Benefits later" (delayed)
